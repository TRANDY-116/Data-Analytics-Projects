Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of loans that could be used to predict their borrower Rate. The main focus was on the Employment Status,Loan Status,IsBorrowerHomeowner and Stated Monthly Income.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consisted of borrower Interest and attributes of 113,937 loans. The attributes included original loan amount, borrower's Prosper rating, loan term, borrower's stated monthly income, as well as many other features such as borrower's employment status, debt to income ratio, current loan status etc. The dataset can be found here, with feature documentation available here
